Week 01

Psalm 23 — The Lord Is My Shepherd

“The Lord is my shepherd; I shall not want.” — Psalm 23:1 (ESV)

Read

Read Psalm 23 slowly (twice if possible).

Reflect

  • Where have you noticed God’s provision this week?
  • What anxiety could you entrust to the Shepherd?
  • How is your soul being restored?

Respond

Pray through the psalm in your own words. Choose one gratitude action for this week.
