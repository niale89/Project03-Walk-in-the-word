Sample Study

Psalm 23: The Lord Is My Shepherd

“The Lord is my shepherd; I shall not want.” — Psalm 23:1 (ESV)

Read

Read Psalm 23 slowly. If you can, read it twice.

Reflect

  • Where have you noticed God’s provision this week?
  • What anxiety might you release by trusting the Shepherd?
  • How does God restore your soul in this season?

Respond

Write a short prayer. Choose one step of gratitude or obedience to practice today.
