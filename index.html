Welcome, Sister

A gentle weekly rhythm to stay rooted in Scripture with one passage, one reflection, and one action.

Start a study

Week 1 — Courage in the Everyday

“Be strong and courageous...” — Joshua 1:9

  • Read: Joshua 1:1–9
  • Reflect: Where do I feel timid this week?
  • Act: One small step of courage by Friday.

Week 2 — Rest for the Weary

“Come to me... and I will give you rest.” — Matthew 11:28

  • Read: Matthew 11:25–30
  • Reflect: What drains me most right now?
  • Act: Schedule one 30-minute quiet block.

Week 3 — Peace in the Storm

“Peace I leave with you...” — John 14:27

  • Read: John 14:25–31
  • Reflect: What anxiety can I hand to God?
  • Act: Write and pray one specific request.
